Where the Light Shines is a game project that was made during the Global Game Jam 2019. Since the theme for GGJ 2019 is “what home means to you,” we decided to make a game about a sailor who is in the middle of the rough seas returning home to his lighthouse. As a sound designer of the team, I have composed original music and created sound effects for the game; as well as making sure that the sound works well in the game. The game won the award for best multiplayer in the Montréal Game Jam 2019 @ ÉTS jam site.
