Assignment 3 - Biography

Sherman Fong’s portfolio conveys a sense of his multi-disciplinary nature as an artist. Graduated in 2020, he have studied in Concordia University; under Specialization in Computation Arts. Throughout his life, he has designed web sites, composed musical work and created sound effects for video game projects and developed interactive installations. His works and contributions have been exhibited and given awards in events such as Concordia’s Year-End D/CART Show of 2018 and Global Game Jam 2019.
