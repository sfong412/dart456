SHERM THE SOUND GUY - NIGHTS INTO DREAMS (2020)

    MATERIALS:
  • Ableton Live

Nights into Dreams is a personal musical project of mine as Sherm the Sound Guy. Nights into Dreams is a plunderphonics album, in which the album is built entirely based on the technique of sampling. The project is an experiment to see if sampling can be done without creating any distinguishable hints about the source material being sampled (which ranges from known acts such as Kim Petras to obscure bands like the Evening Attraction). With influences from various genres such as trip hop and shoegaze, the overall sonic atmosphere of the album is mostly melancholic and nocturnal, perfect for late-night listens.
